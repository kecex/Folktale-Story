Chapter 2

A princess's song echoes through sorrow and hope.

Rejected for her appearance, Dayang Senandung's journey becomes a timeless story of resilience, kindness, and forgiveness.

Long ago, there was a kind princess named Dayang Senandung. She was born with dark skin because of a curse. Although she had a gentle heart, some people judged her only by her appearance.
As she grew older, Dayang Senandung married a prince. However, her mother-in-law disliked her and treated her unfairly. One day, Dayang Senandung had to leave the palace and live in the forest while waiting for her baby to be born.
Even during difficult times, she remained kind, brave, and hopeful. After many challenges, the truth was finally revealed. Her family realized how wrong they had been, and Dayang Senandung was welcomed home with love and respect.

Moral of The Story

  • Never judge someone by their appearance
  • Kindness and courage are true strengths
  • Forgiveness helps heal broken hearts
