Batu Belah Batu Bertangkup reminds us to cherish family with love and kindness.

This beloved folktale tells the story of a magical rock and a mother's love, teaching children the importance of appreciation, respect, and family bonds.

Story motifs

Batu Belah Batu Bertangkup tells of a mother whose sadness grows from feeling unappreciated by the people she loves most.

The magical stone becomes a powerful symbol of regret, reminding listeners to value family before love is lost.
